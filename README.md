## 🏦 For Banks & Investment Companies

A production-grade data engineering platform for real-time trading analytics, risk management, and regulatory compliance.

## 🎯 Business Value

- **Real-time P&L tracking** - Monitor trading profits/losses instantly
- **Risk management** - VaR, Greeks, stress testing calculations
- **Regulatory compliance** - MiFID II, Basel III reporting
- **Market surveillance** - Anomaly detection, trade monitoring
- **Client reporting** - Automated portfolio performance reports
